Math Sciences Entrance Requirements Checklist for students from other PSU campuses

This checklist is designed specifically for students from other campuses of the Penn State system who plan to transfer to the Mathematical Sciences program at Penn State Harrisburg.

A minimum of 60 credit hours with a 2.0 cumulative grade point average are required which are distributed as follows.

Additional requirements  
  • First-Year Seminar, 1 credit of any course with a S, T, X, or PSU designations.
  • United States Cultures and International Cultures Requirements, 3 credits of any course with a US designation and 3 credits of any course with an IL designation. These can be satisfied simultaneously with any of the above requirements, or any course in the degree requirements.
  • Writing Across the Curriculum requirement is satisfied by MATH 311W or MASC 475W, a required course in the MASC degree program.
  • If you spend the first two years at Penn State Harrisburg, you also need to take SSET 295 (1 credit).
