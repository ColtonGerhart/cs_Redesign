Typical Schedule for students admitted to the undergraduate program before Summer 2013

Students are expected to complete the requirements for a BS and an MS degree in five years; proper scheduling is very important to make sure all required courses are taken. A student in the IUG program must work very closely with an adviser to ensure all degree requirements can be met. A typical schedule for a student in the program appears below. Bolded courses are those that satisfy requirements for both the BS and the MS degrees.
