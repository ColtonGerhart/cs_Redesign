Computer Science Entrance Requirements Checklist for students from the Harrisburg Area Community College (HACC)

This check list is designed specifically for students from the Harrisburg Area Community College (HACC) who plan to transfer to the computer science program at Penn State Harrisburg after completing their studies at HACC. All course numbers on this page are HACC course numbers.

A minimum of 56 credit hours with a 2.0 cummulative grade point average are required which are distributed as follows.

Additional requirements
(6 credits)		  
  • First-Year Seminar, 1 credit of any course with a S, T, X, or PSU designations.
  • United States Cultures and International Cultures Requirements, 3 credits of any course with a US designation and 3 credits of any course with an IL designation. These can be satisfied simultaneously with any of the above requirements, or any course in the degree requirements.
  • Writing Across the Curriculum requirement is satisfied by CMPSC 487W, a required course in the COMP degree program.
